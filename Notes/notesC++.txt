3 types of Identifiers

1. Reserved Words

   - Words that have a predefined meaning that CANNOT be changed
   - Example: int

2. Library Identifers

   - Words that have a predefined meaning that CAN be changed
   - Example: sin, cos

3. User-defined Identifiers

   - Words that are created by the user
   - Only letters, digits, and underscore

Self-documenting code

- All user-defined identifiers have a meaning associated in the purpose of that identifer
- Example: largest_sum
